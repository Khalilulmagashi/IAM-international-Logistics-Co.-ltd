About Us

Built for reliability.
Driven by quality.

IAM International Logistics Co., Ltd. is a Nigeria-registered company delivering construction materials and coordinated logistics to sites, contractors, and developers — with the documentation discipline you expect from an international supply partner.

Our team handles procurement, supplier verification, and dispatch scheduling so your project timeline stays predictable: clear paperwork, traceable batches, and responsive coordination from quote to delivery.

From PPE to structural steel, we focus on one outcome — the right materials, on time, with records that stand up to site and audit review.
